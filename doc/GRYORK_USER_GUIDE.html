Platform Overview

Gryork is a bill discounting platform connecting Sub-Contractors with NBFCs for working capital financing against verified invoices.

What is Bill Discounting?

Sub-Contractors often face cash flow issues due to delayed payments. Gryork enables them to get immediate working capital against verified bills, while the EPC company continues its normal payment cycle.

How It Works

Sub-Contractor Submits Bill
EPC Verifies Approves Amount
RMT Reviews Risk Assessment
NBFC Funds Disburses Capital

5

Access Partner Portal

Login to partner.gryork.com to manage CWCRF verifications

Verifying a CWCRF

When verifying a Sub-Contractor's CWC request, provide:

  • Approved CWC Amount - Amount you're willing to back (can be less than requested)
  • Repayment Timeline - Confirm or modify the requested tenure (30/45/60/90 days)
  • Repayment Source - Certified RA bill / Client release / Internal treasury / Retention release

Transaction States

Status Description Next Action
CREATED Transaction created after Sub-Contractor selects NBFC NBFC initiates escrow setup
ESCROW_SETUP Escrow account created for repayment NBFC disburses funds
DISBURSED Funds transferred to Sub-Contractor Await repayment on due date
OVERDUE Payment not received by due date Follow-up & collection actions
REPAID Full repayment received Transaction complete
Overdue Handling

System automatically marks transactions as overdue if repayment is not received within 3 days of due date. Notifications are sent to all parties.
