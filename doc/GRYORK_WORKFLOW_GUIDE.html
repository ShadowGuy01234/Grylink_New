1. Platform Overview

Gryork is a bill discounting platform that connects Sub-Contractors working with EPC (Engineering, Procurement & Construction) companies to NBFCs (Non-Banking Financial Companies) for working capital financing.

What is Bill Discounting?
Sub-Contractors often face cash flow issues due to delayed payments from EPC companies. Gryork enables them to get immediate working capital against their verified bills, while the EPC company continues its normal payment cycle.

Core Concept: CWC (Credit on Working Capital)

The platform facilitates "Credit on Working Capital" - short-term financing against verified invoices/bills submitted by Sub-Contractors.

8. Additional Features

Referral Agent Program

ü§ù
Earn Commissions

Referral agents can introduce EPC companies to Gryork and earn commissions on successful transactions.

Agent Features:

  • Introduce new EPC partners
  • Track introduced EPCs and their Sub-Contractors
  • View commission earnings and payouts
  • Dashboard with performance metrics

Commission Structure:

Commission is calculated as a percentage of platform fees on transactions involving introduced EPCs.

Re-KYC Process

Re-KYC can be triggered for:

  • Annual Renewal - KYC documents expire after 1 year
  • Document Update - When company details change
  • Risk Concern - When NBFC or RMT flags a concern
  • Transaction Dispute - After payment issues
System automatically identifies expiring KYC documents and triggers reminders.

Blacklist Management

The platform maintains a blacklist of entities flagged for fraudulent or unacceptable behavior.

Blacklist Workflow:

  1. Report - Any authorized user can report an entity
  2. Review - Ops/Admin reviews the report
  3. Decision - Approve or reject blacklisting
  4. Enforcement - Blacklisted entities cannot transact
  5. Appeal - Founder can revoke blacklist status
