🔹 Detailed Version (for About Us Page)

Aditya Kumar — Founder, GRYORK

Aditya Kumar is the Founder of GRYORK and a 2nd-year B.Tech student in Computer Science (Data Science) at Ajay Kumar Garg Engineering College, Ghaziabad. Coming from a family deeply rooted in the infrastructure sector, Aditya witnessed firsthand the challenges subcontractors face — especially delayed payments and financial instability.

His father, a subcontractor for over 15 years, has worked on more than 50 projects with 1000+ workers across India. Observing the struggles of workers and subcontractors inspired Aditya to create GRYORK — a platform designed to tackle India’s ₹1.51 lakh crore pending bills crisis, which impacts millions and pulls down the nation’s GDP by 2–3%.

Under his leadership, GRYORK was selected for PW School of Startups Aarambh 7.0, where Aditya was honored with the Best Founder Award. He is passionate about problem-solving, leadership, and empowering others to create real impact.

Motto: “Be your own hero.”
